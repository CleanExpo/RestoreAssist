# ICP & positioning — Unite-Group

## Primary ICP

**Australian trade and restoration operators** running field + office on fragmented tools:

- Owner-operator or small team (2–30 staff)
- High compliance pressure (insurance, WHS, documentation)
- Losing margin to admin drag, rework, and “another app” fatigue

## Jobs to be done

1. See jobs, people, and money in one place — without re-keying between field and desk.
2. Produce claim-ready, insurer-aligned documentation faster than spreadsheets + Word.
3. Adopt AI **without** generic chatbots that hallucinate scope or ignore audit trails.

## Positioning statement

**Unite-Group** builds **production-grade operator systems** for trades — not demo apps. Nexus Hub coordinates portfolio delivery; products like **RestoreAssist** (restoration), **NRPG**, **Disaster Recovery**, and **ATO-APP** serve specific verticals under one governance model.

## Differentiators

| vs generic SaaS | vs “AI wrapper” agencies |
|-----------------|-------------------------|
| Field-first workflows, AU compliance baked in | Agentic OS with wiki, board gate, Linear truth |
| Sibling products under one parent — shared ops learning | Hermes + Margot + Pi — human CEO still owns strategy |

## Anti-positioning (do not claim)

- We are not a low-code agency selling one-off websites only.
- RestoreAssist is not “the whole group” — it is the restoration vertical.
- We do not promise full autonomy without human sign-off on external-facing work.

## Offers (high level)

| Layer | Audience message |
