Every new RestoreAssist account starts on a 15-day trial. To keep using inspections, AI reports, and integrations after that, pick a paid plan.

<VideoExplainer slug="help-billing" />

## When does the trial end

The trial runs for 15 days from signup. Three days before expiry you'll see a banner at the top of the dashboard. After day 15, your subscription status flips to `CANCELED` and AI-backed actions return HTTP 402 until you upgrade. Inspections you've already created stay readable.

## Open the upgrade page

Click the **Upgrade** button in the dashboard top bar, or go directly to `/billing/upgrade`.

## Pick a tier

| Tier           | Price        | Best for                                                                                        |
| -------------- | ------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **STANDARD**   | $99 / month  | Solo tradie · 1 user · unlimited inspections · standard AI reports                              |
| **PREMIUM**    | $199 / month | Small crew · up to 5 users · premium AI model routing · Xero + MYOB integrations · BYOK support |
| **ENTERPRISE** | Contact us   | Multi-site or franchise · SSO · custom integrations · dedicated support                         |

All prices are AUD and include 10% GST per Australian Tax Office rules. Invoices are issued monthly to the ABN on file.

## Checkout

Click **Upgrade to STANDARD** (or PREMIUM). RestoreAssist sends you to Stripe Checkout. After payment your subscription status flips to `ACTIVE` immediately — no waiting on webhooks for the UI to unlock.

## What happens to my data if the trial expires

- Inspections and reports already created remain in your account and stay readable
- New AI generations are blocked (HTTP 402) until you upgrade
- After 90 days of `CANCELED` status, RestoreAssist sends a deletion notice; data is purged 30 days later unless you reactivate

## Cancel or downgrade

From **Settings → Billing → Manage subscription** you can cancel or switch tiers. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Downgrades from PREMIUM to STANDARD pro-rate the difference as account credit.
