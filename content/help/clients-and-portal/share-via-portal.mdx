The client portal gives your homeowner a read-only view of the signed-off report and any invoices you've attached. Tokens are revocable and rotatable from the admin side — the client never logs in.

<VideoExplainer slug="help-clients-and-portal" />

## When to use the portal

Use the portal once an inspection is `COMPLETED` and the report is signed off. Before sign-off the document is still a draft; don't share drafts via the portal.

## Provision the client portal account

From the inspection detail page, click **Share with client** in the action bar. If the homeowner doesn't already have a `ClientPortalAccount`, RestoreAssist creates one tied to the inspection.

## Send the link

The portal link looks like `https://restoreassist.app/portal/[token]`. You can:

- Copy and paste it into your own email or SMS
- Click **Send via email** to use RestoreAssist's templated email
- Generate a QR code to hand the client at handover

The token is opaque and unguessable. There is no public listing of portal accounts.

## What the client sees

When the client opens the link, they see:

- The signed-off report (read-only PDF + inline web view)
- Any attached invoices and their payment status
- Photos with captions (chain-of-custody hashes are hidden from the client UI)
- A contact button that emails you back

## Rotate or revoke access

From **Settings → Client portal accounts** an admin can:

- **Rotate token** — invalidates the old link and issues a fresh one
- **Revoke** — disables the account; the link returns a 410 Gone

Rotate after any incident (forwarded email, lost phone, ownership change). Rotation does not change the report itself, only the URL.
