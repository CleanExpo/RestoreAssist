## Audience

Primary ICP:

- Australian restoration owner-operators
- 1-5 technician businesses
- Water damage primary; mould, fire/smoke, storm damage secondary
- Uses or has considered ServiceM8, Ascora, Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks, Word/PDF report workflows
- Works nights/weekends on report admin
- Cares about IICRC S500, WHS, Australian Building Code references, GST, ABN, clean reporting, photo/data capture, and insurer-ready documentation

Secondary audience:

- Lead technicians who care about mobile field capture
- Bookkeepers/accountants who care about clean invoice and line-item handoff
- Assessors/claims-adjacent people who influence report expectations but are not the v1 buyer

Anti-audience:

- Homeowners
- US restoration operators
- Large franchises with procurement/central IT
- General builders doing occasional water work

## Creative guardrails

Do:

- Use direct, grounded founder voice.
- Speak to operators who are already under pressure.
- Say "AI assists" not "AI replaces".
- Use Australian specifics: ABN, GST, IICRC S500, WHS, Australian Building Code, AEST.
- Invite trial and feedback rather than hype.

Do not:

- Promise insurer acceptance.
- Promise compliance outcomes.
- Promise faster payment.
- Say "revolutionary", "game-changing", "best-in-class", "seamless", "unlock", "leverage", or "world-class".
- Post the same Facebook copy into multiple groups on the same day.
- Publish group posts without checking group rules.
