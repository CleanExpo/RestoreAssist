RestoreAssist is the Australian-built CRM and compliance platform purpose-made for water-damage restoration technicians.

If you're a sole-trader or small restoration business that hates re-typing the same job details into five different apps, RestoreAssist is for you. We pull your business profile straight from your ABN, generate IICRC S500-compliant inspection reports from your on-site photos, and keep the paper trail that insurers, loss adjusters, and Queensland / NSW / Vic building regulators actually ask for.

What you get:

• ABN-anchored setup — type your ABN once, we hydrate business name, ACN, GST status, state, address, pricing defaults from the Australian Business Register. No more re-keying.

• IICRC S500:2021 compliant reports — every report cites the correct edition and section. Generated by AI from photos, moisture readings, and your scope notes. Editable before you send.

• Chain-of-custody photo capture — every photo carries a SHA-256 manifest plus UTC timestamp, GPS coordinates, device hint, and your user hash. If an insurer challenges your evidence, it holds up.

• Australian-specific compliance — 10% GST, 11-digit ABN validation, state-specific building codes (QLD QBCC, NSW Fair Trading, VIC VBA, etc.).

• Bring-your-own-keys — connect your existing Xero, MYOB, QuickBooks, ServiceM8, or Ascora account. We sync; we don't replace.

• Cloud storage your way — Google Drive (live), OneDrive (coming), or keep everything local. Your client data stays where you choose.

• Tradie evidence-capture FAB — one-tap photo while you're crawling under a slab. Auto-tags moisture zones, location, and timestamp.

• AI Sidekick (in development) — voice-guided technician assistant for on-site scope confirmation, IICRC method recommendation, and WHS hazard flagging.
